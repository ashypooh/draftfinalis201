The Lore of Pantlaza

Pantlaza, the Sun-Favored, is a legendary commander known for his divine connection to the sun. He empowers his allies with radiant energy, bringing strength and hope to his followers on the battlefield.

Pantlaza's Abilities

  1. Sun-Blessed Resilience
    • All creatures under his control gain +1/+1 when they are healed.
    • Creatures with lifelink receive an additional bonus to attack.
  2. Solar Strike
    • Unleashes a beam of radiant energy, dealing double damage to shadow creatures.
    • Each time a sun-related card is played, Pantlaza gains additional power.
  3. Sunlit Revival
    • At the beginning of your upkeep, you may return one creature from your graveyard to the battlefield.
    • This ability only triggers if at least one sun-aligned card is in play.

Mastering Pantlaza's Strategy

Building a deck around Pantlaza, the Sun-Favored, requires careful planning. Utilize lifelink creatures and spells that trigger healing to activate his powerful abilities. Cards that deal with graveyard recursion will allow you to make the most of his Sunlit Revival ability.
